Overview

Thank you for your assistance with this project! As a professional designer, your insight is invaluable to this research project. We very much appreciate your input.

First, you will be asked some basic questions about yourself and your background as a designer.

Next, we ask you to arrange various parts of an Airline Kiosk Check-In procedure. To do so, you will drag and drop various parts of the Kiosk Check-In procedure to create a final product. Certain parts must go before or after other parts, and you will be alerted if these need to be changed. When you are done, click "Complete" in the upper right hand.

Again, thank your for being a part of our research project!


This research project has been approved by the designated ethics officer in the Computer Science Department at UCL. The research project is compliant with the Data Protection Act 1998 (Data Protection Registration Number: 20150722)
